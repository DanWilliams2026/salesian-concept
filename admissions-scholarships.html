Scholarships & Bursaries

Supporting families

A Salesian education for every talented student

Salesian College believes that ability and character — not financial means — should determine whether a young person joins us. We offer a range of scholarships and means-tested bursaries to help talented students from all backgrounds access the education they deserve.

Academic Scholarship

Awarded to outstanding candidates in the 11+ Entrance Assessment. Up to 10% fee reduction, with prestige and recognition within the school community.

Award: up to 10% of fees

Music Scholarship

For students with exceptional musical ability. Audition-based. Includes instrumental lessons as part of the scholarship package.

Award: up to 10% of fees + music tuition

Sports Scholarship

For boys who demonstrate outstanding sporting ability. Assessed via sport-specific trials and interview.

Award: up to 10% of fees

Means-tested support

Bursaries for families in need

Our bursary programme provides means-tested financial assistance for families who could not otherwise afford Salesian fees. Bursaries are assessed annually and can provide significant fee remission — in some cases up to 100% of tuition fees for families who demonstrate exceptional financial need.

All bursary applications are confidential. We encourage any family in financial difficulty to contact the Headmaster's office.

All bursary applications are handled in strict confidence

Reviewed annually based on updated family circumstances

Remission from 10% to 100% depending on assessed need
