Life at Salesian College

School Life

A school that never stands still

At Salesian College, education goes far beyond the classroom. From the sporting fields to the stage, from international expeditions to local charitable work, our pupils are encouraged to explore every opportunity and discover who they truly are.

Sport

Excellence on the field & court

Sport is at the heart of Salesian College. From competitive fixtures to recreational play, we offer something for every student — whether they aspire to represent the county or simply enjoy being active.

Our facilities include a state-of-the-art 4G pitch, cricket nets, and tennis courts. We compete at the highest level in local, regional, and national competitions.

Performing Arts

Find your voice on stage

Our dedicated Music School features an auditorium and individual practice rooms, while our Drama Studio offers space for performance and rehearsal. Every year, the college stages major productions that draw full audiences from across the community.

From GCSE and A-Level Music and Drama to informal concerts and open-mic events, there are countless ways for students to discover and develop their creative talents.

Music

Orchestra, choir, ensembles and individual instrumental tuition

Drama

Residential Visits

Learning beyond the classroom

We believe in the power of experience outside the school gates. Our extensive programme of educational visits takes students from the French Alps to the streets of Rome, from local battlefield tours to charitable expeditions.

Annual highlights include the Year 9 Lourdes pilgrimage, ski trips, language exchanges, and GCSE and A-Level subject visits. Each trip is carefully designed to enrich the curriculum and broaden horizons.
