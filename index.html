The Salesian Promise

"Every young person has a gift.
Our job is to help them find it."

Founded by the Salesians of Don Bosco in 1901, we believe in the potential of every student. Our approach is simple: know each boy by name, meet him where he is, and never stop believing in who he could become.

01

Academic Rigour

Around 10 students per class from Year 7 to A-level. Every pupil seen, stretched, and supported — every single lesson.

02

Community & Care

The Salesian tradition places the relationship between teacher and student at the heart of everything. No boy falls through the cracks.

03

Faith & Character

Rooted in reason, religion, and loving kindness — we graduate confident, compassionate young men ready for the world beyond school.

Life at Salesian

Sport. Arts. Faith. Community.

Over 40 clubs and societies, from football pitches to performing arts stages, from Lourdes pilgrimages to Duke of Edinburgh expeditions. Every student finds his place — and his passion.
