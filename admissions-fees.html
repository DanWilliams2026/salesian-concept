School Fees

Fees overview

Transparent, fair pricing

Salesian College is committed to making our excellent education accessible. Our fees are among the most competitive of independent grammar schools in the South of England, and we offer a range of scholarships and bursaries for families who need financial support.

All fees are per term and are reviewed annually. The tuition fee covers all timetabled lessons, use of facilities, and the school's iPad programme. Additional charges apply for co-curricular activities, trips, and examinations.
