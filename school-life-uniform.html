Second Hand Uniform

PTA uniform shop

Pre-loved uniform at great prices

The Salesian PTA runs a thriving second-hand uniform shop, offering blazers, ties, sports kits, and other uniform items in excellent condition at a fraction of the original price. This service benefits both families looking to reduce costs and those who wish to donate items their son has grown out of.

The shop is run by parent volunteers and opens at set times throughout the school year — including at key transition points (July and August) when many families are sourcing uniform for the first time.

Opening times
Advertised in the weekly school newsletter and on the school social media pages. Check for pop-up sale dates each term.
What's available
Blazers, ties, PE kit, trousers, and Salesian sports items. Stock varies — first come, first served.
Donating items
Clean, labelled items in good condition can be dropped at the school office during term time. All donations are gratefully received.
New uniform

New uniform is available from our official uniform supplier. The full uniform list is sent to families of all new students in July. Salesian-branded items (blazer, tie, PE kit) must be purchased from the school supplier.

Ask the school office
Uniform policy

All students are expected to wear the correct Salesian uniform at all times during the school day. The uniform policy is detailed in the parent handbook, available from the school office and the VLE.
