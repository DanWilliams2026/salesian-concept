A-Level Subjects

A wide choice

Over 25 subjects to choose from

Salesian College offers a broad and ambitious A-level curriculum. Students typically study three A-levels over two years, choosing from our full subject menu. Small class sizes mean individual attention from specialist teachers — and the freedom to pursue the combination of subjects that best suits their future ambitions.
