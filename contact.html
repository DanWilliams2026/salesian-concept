Location & Directions

Finding Salesian College

Salesian College is located on Reading Road, Farnborough, just a short distance from Farnborough town centre and Farnborough Main railway station.

By rail

Farnborough Main station (London Waterloo ”“ approximately 45 minutes) is a 15-minute walk from the school. Trains run frequently throughout the day.

By car

From the M3 (junction 4), take the A325 northbound. Follow signs for Farnborough. The school is on Reading Road (B3014). Visitor parking is available on site.

By bus

Several Stagecoach bus routes serve Reading Road. Please check the Stagecoach South website for current timetables.
