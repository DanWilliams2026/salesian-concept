Digital Learning

Our digital environment

Technology that empowers learning

Digital technology is embedded in every aspect of the learning experience at Salesian College. As an Apple Distinguished School, we use a 1:1 iPad programme, a school-wide VLE, and a suite of digital tools that enhance teaching, support independent learning, and connect students and parents to the school community.

Our approach is always pedagogy-led — technology serves learning, not the other way around. Every tool we deploy is chosen because it helps students learn better, not simply because it is new.

Core digital tools

1:1 iPad programme

Every student from Year 7 has their own iPad, managed by school IT. The device is used in every subject for research, creation, and collaboration.

FrogLearn VLE

Our Virtual Learning Environment gives students 24/7 access to homework, resources, timetables, and teacher feedback. Parents can log in to track progress.

Apple Classroom

Teachers use Apple Classroom to manage iPads in real time — sharing resources, monitoring activity, and guiding students through tasks seamlessly.

Swift Playgrounds & coding

All students learn to code through Apple's Swift Playgrounds, developing logical thinking and digital fluency from Year 7 onwards.
