The Governing Body

Governance

Guiding the College forward

The Governing Body of Salesian College is responsible for the strategic direction and oversight of the school. Governors work closely with the Headmaster and senior leadership team to ensure the College fulfils its mission, maintains its standards, and acts in the best interests of its students and community.

Responsibilities

What governors do

Strategic direction

Setting the vision and values of the school and overseeing the implementation of its development plan.

Financial oversight

Ensuring the school is financially sustainable and that resources are used effectively in the interests of students.

Safeguarding

Holding the school to account for the welfare and safety of all its students and staff.

Standards & accountability

Monitoring the quality of education and holding the Headmaster and senior team to account for pupil outcomes.
