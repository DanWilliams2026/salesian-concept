The Salesian Difference

Founded by the Salesians of Don Bosco, our school is built on a simple belief: that every boy thrives when he feels known, valued, and challenged in the right way.

We combine rigorous academics with a genuine community — one where your son won't get lost in the crowd, and where staff truly know every student by name.

The result is young men who leave Salesian not just with excellent grades, but with the character, confidence, and values to go further in life.
