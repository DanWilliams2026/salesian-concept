The Salesian Difference

Why Salesian

A school unlike any other

There are many fine schools. But Salesian College offers something genuinely distinctive — an education rooted in a 120-year tradition, delivered in small classes by expert teachers, within a community that knows and cares for every individual student.

100% pass rate

Every student achieves at GCSE. Our results consistently place us among the top independent schools in Hampshire.

~10 per class

Small class sizes mean every student is seen, known, and stretched. Teaching is genuinely personalised.

Apple Distinguished

Recognised nationally for innovation in digital learning. Every student learns with an iPad from Day 1.

Catholic ethos

Faith, service, and values are not add-ons here — they are woven into every day of school life.

Single-sex 11”“16

Research consistently shows that boys thrive academically in a single-sex environment in their secondary years.

Great location

Located in Farnborough, easily accessible from across Hampshire and Surrey with excellent transport links.

Pastoral excellence

ISI inspectors rated pupils' personal development as "outstanding." Every boy is known and supported.

Co-ed Sixth Form

The Sixth Form opens to boys and girls — providing a natural transition to the diversity of university life.

Sporting excellence

Competitive success across football, rugby, cricket, athletics and more — with an ethos that every student participates.
