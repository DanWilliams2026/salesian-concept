Beyond the syllabus

Extended Learning

Stretch & enrichment

Learning that goes further

Salesian College is committed to stretching every student beyond the requirements of the examination syllabus. Our extended learning programme challenges the most able students, encourages intellectual curiosity across all year groups, and provides pathways to national competitions, awards, and extra qualifications.

Duke of Edinburgh's Award

We offer Bronze, Silver, and Gold DofE to students in Years 9â€“12. The award develops teamwork, leadership, perseverance, and community spirit through volunteering, physical activity, skill development, and expedition.

CREST Awards

CREST is the British Science Association's nationally recognised award scheme for student-led science, technology, engineering, and maths projects. Students can achieve Bronze, Silver, and Gold CREST qualifications.

Olympiad competitions

Our students compete in national Olympiad competitions including the UK Maths Trust, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics Olympiads. Many earn certificates and medals at Senior and Junior level.

Reading & super-curricular

All departments provide reading lists and super-curricular recommendations for students aiming for competitive university entry. Academic staff run extension sessions, lecture series, and discussion groups throughout the year.

Language awards

Students can sit the DELF (French) and DELE (Spanish) internationally recognised language qualifications alongside their GCSE and A-level courses, demonstrating proficiency to future employers and universities.
