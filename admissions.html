Year 7 Entry

Joining us at 11+

Our main entry point is Year 7 at age 11. We look for boys who will thrive in and contribute to the Salesian community — academically able, enthusiastic, and ready to embrace everything school life has to offer.

After registering, pupils sit an entrance examination. Successful candidates are invited for an informal interview with the Deputy Headteacher before receiving an offer.

Years 8”“10

In-year entry

Limited places may be available in Years 8, 9, and 10. Families should contact the Admissions Secretary to express interest. Where a vacancy exists, candidates will be invited to visit the school, attend an interview, and sit an age-appropriate entrance examination.

Please note: The College is unable to consider new students for Year 11.

Sixth Form

Year 12 entry

Our co-educational Sixth Form welcomes both Salesian students continuing their studies and external candidates from other schools. The application process involves a subject choices form, personal statement, and interview with school leadership.

Provisional offers are made by half term in the spring term. A minimum of five GCSEs at grade 6 or above is typically required.

Scholarships & Bursaries

Supporting exceptional talent

We are committed to making a Salesian education accessible to talented boys regardless of financial background. Scholarships are awarded on merit; bursaries are means-tested and can cover a significant proportion of fees.

Scholarships are available in: Academic, Sport, Music, and All-Round excellence. All scholarship candidates are assessed as part of the normal admissions process.

Fees

Investing in your son's future

Our fees reflect the exceptional quality of education, facilities, and pastoral care we provide. For the most up-to-date fee schedule, please contact the Bursar's office directly.

Fees are reviewed annually and published each September for the following academic year. Termly payment plans are available, and a number of scholarships and bursaries can reduce the cost significantly for eligible families.
