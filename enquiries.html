Our Sixth Form offers a wide range of A-Level subjects in a supportive, ambitious environment. We welcome applications from both current Salesian students and external candidates.

Entry is based on GCSE results and a personal interview. We look for students who are motivated, curious, and ready to take the next step in their academic journey.
