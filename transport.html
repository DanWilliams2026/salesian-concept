Transport & Travel

Getting to Salesian

Well connected

Salesian College is situated in Farnborough, Hampshire — a highly accessible location for families across north Hampshire, Surrey, and Berkshire. The school is served by coach routes, public buses, and Farnborough railway station (direct trains to London Waterloo in under an hour).

School coach service

Dedicated coach routes

The school operates a dedicated coach service covering several routes. Coaches run every school day and return after the school day ends (and on some evenings for extra-curricular activities).

Route 1 — Fleet / Church Crookham

Via Fleet town centre and Church Crookham

Route 2 — Aldershot / Farnham

Via Aldershot town centre and Farnham

Route 3 — Camberley / Sandhurst

Serving Surrey border communities

Route 4 — Hook / Basingstoke

Via Hook village and North Hants corridor

Full timetables and stop locations are available from the school office. Contact office@salesiancollege.com for pricing and booking.

By train

Farnborough Main station is a 15-minute walk from the school. Direct services from London Waterloo (50 mins), Basingstoke (15 mins), and Guildford (20 mins). Many students use the train daily.
