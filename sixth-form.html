Sixth Form Life

Where boys become young adults

Our co-educational Sixth Form is a vibrant, purposeful environment that prepares students for university and beyond. With average class sizes of just 10, every student receives the individual attention they need to excel at A-Level.

As the only independent all-boys school in the area with a co-educational Sixth Form, we offer a genuinely unique experience — the strong Salesian community of the lower school, enriched by the diversity of our Sixth Form intake.

Students are supported through a dedicated Leadership & Personal Development programme, a Salesian Networking & Careers programme, and a rich programme of enrichment activities alongside their studies.

What makes us different

The Salesian Sixth Form advantage

Distinctive 4-subject offer

Students select four subjects at AS Level, keeping options open before focusing on three at A2 — providing breadth and flexibility.

Careers & networking

Our Salesian Networking & Careers programme connects students with professionals, universities, and alumni across a wide range of fields.

Leadership development

Every Sixth Former participates in our Leadership & Personal Development programme, building skills that extend far beyond the A-Level classroom.

Co-educational environment

A co-educational Sixth Form prepares students for the diversity of university and professional life after single-sex education.

100% UCAS success

Every student who applies to university through UCAS receives an offer — a testament to our outstanding guidance and preparation.

Salesian pastoral care

The warmth and care of the Salesian community continues into the Sixth Form — every student has a dedicated tutor and is known by name.

Pastoral Care

Supported every step of the way

The transition to Sixth Form is significant — academically, personally, and socially. Our pastoral team is dedicated to supporting every student through this transition and beyond.

Each student has a personal tutor who meets with them regularly, monitors their academic progress, and supports their wellbeing. A dedicated Sixth Form team provides guidance on UCAS applications, personal statements, and university choices.

Dedicated personal tutor for every student
Full UCAS and personal statement guidance
University visits and open day support
Wellbeing support and mental health resources
Oxbridge and medical school preparation
Enrichment

Beyond the A-Level

A Salesian Sixth Form education is about far more than exam results. Our enrichment programme develops the whole person — building confidence, leadership, and a genuine readiness for adult life.

How to Apply

Ready to join our Sixth Form?

Applications for Year 12 entry open in the autumn term. The process involves a subject choices form, personal statement, and an interview with school leadership. Provisional offers are made before the spring half-term.

Minimum five GCSEs at grade 6 or above
Grades 7”“9 typically required in chosen subjects
Open to boys and girls from all schools
