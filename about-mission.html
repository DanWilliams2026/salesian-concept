Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

Educating the whole person

Salesian College is a Catholic independent grammar school for boys, dedicated to providing an education of the highest quality within a caring and values-led community. Our mission is rooted in the educational philosophy of St John Bosco, who believed that education should serve the whole person — head, heart, and hands.

We seek to develop young men of integrity, compassion, and ambition: boys who are academically accomplished, spiritually grounded, and equipped to make a positive contribution to the world around them.

The Preventive System

Three pillars of Salesian education

Don Bosco's Preventive System — his method of education — rests on three interlocking principles that continue to shape every aspect of school life at Salesian College.

01

Reason

We engage with every pupil's intelligence and treat them as thinking, capable individuals. Rules and expectations are explained and justified, not simply imposed. We build trust through dialogue.

02

Religion

Faith is woven into the fabric of school life. Chapel, retreat days, the annual Lourdes pilgrimage, and Religious Studies are all expressions of the spiritual dimension of a Salesian education.

03

Loving Kindness

Every student is known, seen, and valued. The warmth of the Salesian community — what Don Bosco called amorevolezza — is felt from the first day at the school to the last.

Our Values

What we stand for

Academic Excellence

We hold high expectations for every student and celebrate achievement in all its forms. Academic rigour sits alongside pastoral care.

Pastoral Care

Every pupil is known by name. Our staff genuinely care about the wellbeing and flourishing of each individual student.

Catholic Ethos

Our Catholic identity is not peripheral but central. It shapes our ethics, our relationships, and our sense of purpose and vocation.

Service to Others

Don Bosco founded his work for the poorest of young people. That spirit of service — to community, to the Church, to the world — remains at the heart of Salesian education today.

Our Mission Statement

"To provide the highest quality of education in a caring Christian community where each person is valued, encouraged to develop their full potential, and inspired to serve God and neighbour."
