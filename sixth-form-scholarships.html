Sixth Form Scholarships

Supporting exceptional students

Recognising talent and potential

Salesian College awards a number of Sixth Form scholarships each year to reward exceptional academic achievement and potential. We also offer means-tested bursaries to ensure that financial circumstances do not prevent a talented student from joining our Sixth Form.

Academic Scholarship

Up to 10% fee remission

Awarded to external candidates who demonstrate outstanding academic potential. Assessed on predicted or achieved GCSE results and a scholarship assessment day.

Music Scholarship

Fee remission + music tuition support

Available to students with exceptional musical ability. Candidates audition before the Head of Music and are assessed on performance, sight-reading, and musical knowledge.

Bursaries

Means-tested bursary support

We believe that a Salesian education should be accessible to any family with a talented child, regardless of financial circumstances. Means-tested bursaries can provide significant fee reduction for families who qualify, assessed on a confidential financial means review.
